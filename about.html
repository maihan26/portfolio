My story

I work at the intersection of geospatial analysis, remote sensing, and research for environmental and social impact. I care about using maps, data, and clear communication to help people understand complex issues and act on them.

My background includes social impact consulting, sustainability, and applied analytics in Vietnam, Sweden, Maldives, and Canada. I am especially interested in projects where spatial thinking supports conservation, climate action, and practical decision-making.

I enjoy building work that is both rigorous and approachable: strong enough for technical audiences, but clear enough for broader communication.
